# AI-Powered Builder Selection

The AI-Powered Builder Selection is the initial step in your creative process. From the landing page, you can choose the type of project you want to build.

## How it Works

1.  **Navigate to the Landing Page**: When you first arrive at the application, you'll see a prominent dropdown menu.
2.  **Select Your Builder**: Click on the dropdown menu to reveal the available builder options:
    *   **dApp Builder**: For creating decentralized applications.
    *   **NFT Builder**: For creating non-fungible tokens and collections.
    *   **Cryptocurrency Builder**: For creating your own cryptocurrency.
3.  **Get Started**: Once you've made your selection, you'll be directed to the corresponding AI-driven builder page, where you can begin creating your project.
