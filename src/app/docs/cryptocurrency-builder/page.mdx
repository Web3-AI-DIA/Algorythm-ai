# Cryptocurrency Builder

Our AI-powered Cryptocurrency Builder helps you create your own cryptocurrency.

## Features

*   **Powered by Gemini-2.5-Flash**: The Cryptocurrency Builder uses **Gemini-2.5-Flash** to guide you through the process of creating your cryptocurrency.
*   **Guided Deployment System**: The Cryptocurrency Builder provides a guided deployment system to help you deploy your cryptocurrency to the blockchain.
*   **Checklist Steps**: The Cryptocurrency Builder provides a checklist of steps to guide you through the process of creating and deploying your cryptocurrency.
