# Credit System

Our application uses a credit-based system to provide you with access to our AI-powered builders.

## How it Works

When you sign up, you'll receive a number of free credits to get you started. Once you've used your free credits, you can purchase more credits or subscribe to a monthly plan.

## Purchasing Credits

We offer several credit packs to suit your needs:

*   **40 credits for $25**
*   **100 credits for $50**
*   **250 credits for $100**

## Subscriptions

For unlimited access to all of our builders, you can subscribe to our monthly plan for **$500/month**.

## Payment Methods

We accept payments through **Stripe** and **NOWPayments**, giving you a range of options to choose from.
